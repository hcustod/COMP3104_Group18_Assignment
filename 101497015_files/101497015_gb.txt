George Brown College Information

George Brown College is one of the largest colleges in Toronto. It offers over 170 full time programs and has over 50,000 overall students. 
It has three major campuses, but several other buildings owned throughout the city. Its major campuses are St. James, Casa Loma, and Waterfront. 

The college has fostered a reputation for being one of the best schools for hands on learning, and as such many graduates go on to find employment
before even graduating from their programs.
