Importance of DevOps in the Software Development Cycle

In the Software development life cycle (SDLC), devops is seen as essential as it allows smoother development by filling in the gap between development and operation teams.
One of the core responsibilities of DevOps is to ensure reliable and continuous deployments of developments, through IAC (Infrastructure as Code) and CI/CD practices.  

Furthermore those that work with DevOps are often responsible for testing, to ensure reliability and consistency in the application.
